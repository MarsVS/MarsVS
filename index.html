Pets for Adoption

Microchipping

Microchipping provides a permanent and reliable method of identifying your pet if they ever get lost. A small, safe chip is placed under the skin and contains a unique ID number linked to your contact details. This greatly increases the chances of reunification and offers peace of mind for responsible pet owners.
